House Style Notes, aka I am NOT just using Dehancer:
My grade pipeline is based on Steve Yedlin's display prep demo although I do disagree with his final goal getting a completely convincing film look. Too nostalgia driven for my taste. I prefer to emulate the "color science" of celluloid alone while leaving grain, halation and gate weave out but those can be added quite easily upon request.

The recipe I'm using is decidedly NOT a plugin like Dehancer, FilmBox, Cineprint etc. Those are fine at mimicing the degraded qualities of film but their color looks more like simple tone curves based on film stocks opposed to a proper, robust, 3D transform the way Yedlin lays out in the video above. The pipeline I use is built around a transform made by Matthias Stoopman based on Kodak 5219 500t. Here it is side by side with the film stock.
